About Us

Living rich, savouring everything was a full-time occupation in the erstwhile province of Awadh. The baankas (gentlemen) and the begums (ladies) made sure each season and nuance produced richness in all aspects of life. Where Lucknow gave us tehzeeb (etiquette), nafasat (delicate style) and chikan embroidery, it also made an occupation out of eating.
